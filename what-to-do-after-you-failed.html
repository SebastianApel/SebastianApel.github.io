Getting back on the horse

Jun 4, 2018

Over the last couple four days, I’ve not been writing. There was a public holiday, I was outside of my usual environment because I was travelling with my family. I did not have a good topic to write about. Many reasons.

Actually, I am very unhappy about that. I feel bad, because I have not made any progress. So, what can you do in such a situation?

The best way I can think of is derived from horseback riding: If you have fallen of the horse, you need to get back it. That’s basically it. Not a long story. Nothing fancy. Just get back up and write your 100 words.

And you should act as your personal horseback riding teacher. A good teacher will not shame you, because you have fallen of the horse. He will help you understand why you have fallen off, and he will motivate you to get back on the horse.

It is a better approach anyway: I cannot change the last couple of days, but I can make sure that today is different.
