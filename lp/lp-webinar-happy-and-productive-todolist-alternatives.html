Does your todo-list stress you?

Mine stresses me. At least it did in the past. Here are a couple of observations, thoughts and feelings I have had around this:

  • When I look at that looong list, I think “I’ll never be able to do everything I SHOULD be doing, or that I WOULD LIKE to do” - and I get frustrated and scared

  • At the end of the day, I am thinking “OMG, so little progress - why didn’t you accomplish more today” - not something I want in the future

  • Or, alternatively, I look at my watch and think “OMG, where has the time gone?” and get frustrated, because it feels like I have “wasted” precious time.

  • Another oldie-but-goodie is spoiling a perfectly productive day when the random thought about another project pops up - and I think “Oh sh*t, I wanted to work on that, too”…

In a nutshell, I have too much stuff I WANT to do, and NOT ENOUGH TIME to do it.

This will never change - and you need to deal with it…

One major insight I had was: THAT will never change. I will always have more stuff I want to do than time doing it. So I better find a way to deal with that situation effectively.

And because of this, I am working on a framework & system to train myself to a different approach.

Wouldn’t it be awesome to be able to relax?

Wouldn’t it be awesome to have a framework & system:

  • that helps you think about all areas of my life
  • helps you make conscious decisions where I want to allocate my time
  • and in that time helps you to prioritize on the work that has the biggist impact in you life
  • so that, at the end of the day, you can be sure you made the best use of your short time on this planet?
