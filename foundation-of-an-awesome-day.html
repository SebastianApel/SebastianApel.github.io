How to Lay the Foundation for an Awesome Day

May 25, 2018

In my last posts on habit formation, I have mentioned the Stanford University psychologist BJ Fogg. Another gold nugget I have gotten from him is his suggestion for an early morning habit that can lay the foundation for an awesome day. He calls it the MauiHabit.

The suggestion is to create the following habit:

When I get out of my bed, and my feet touch the ground,
I will say to myself ‘Today is going to be an awesome day’.

Yes, I can understand if you think “Really? That’s it?”. I would have thought the same. But: I have tried his suggestion for the last couple of days: I was easy to do, it has put a smile on my face. That for itself could be enough, but on top of that, I have had a couple of awesome days this week.

So, I am not asking you to believe me. I am asking you to believe your own experience. Try this for a couple of days - and let me know how it works for you via Twitter.
