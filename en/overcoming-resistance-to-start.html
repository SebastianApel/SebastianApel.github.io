100 words on … overcoming resistance to start publishing

May 23, 2018

I have been struggling with my objective to start publishing content on the Internet now for a while. I’ve tried a lot of things: Setting up habits to write every morning, reading a lot on how to do things. But in the end, I have not been able to establish something regular, much less publish ANYTHING.

Yesterday, I have listened to a podcast with BJ Fogg and David Kadavy: They were talking about how to make it simple enough to just do it. And David said that he gave himself permission to suck. So, I am resetting my bar to just publish 100 words for now. Without any wanting it to be great, awesome, new, innovative, value-adding. Just starting.

Because it is the only way to learn and get better.

I want to read more!
