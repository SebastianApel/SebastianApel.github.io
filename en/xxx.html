Am amazing improvement

So, I have abandoned the “classical” Todo-List for the sake of a this end-of-day ritual, a “backlog” and “the pull principle”. I use the “backlog” and “the pull principle” in the morning (you will find more on those in follow-up posts), and the “Achievement and Reflection” journal in the evening.

To me, this has made a huge difference. I can now look at the day more objectively and go home happy :-) This then helps me to rest and relax better, which in turn improves my happiness and effectiveness the next day.

The hardest part for me was to establish the habit - and I will go more into details in another post.
