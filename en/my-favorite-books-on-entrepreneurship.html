Oct 30, 2018 • Sebastian Apel

This is a start. I will add more books over time.

The Lean Startup

by Eric Ries

This book is one of the “foundational” works in the startup scene.

Eric Ries combines “Lean Production” methodologies with “Startups”, and seeks to provide methodology and framework to improve startup success.

It builds on the work of Steve Blank.

You can get the book here at Amazon.
